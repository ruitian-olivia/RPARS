About Us

The database RPARS

The mission of RPARS is to provide a high-quality and freely accessible resource of antibiotic-resistant strains and their ribosomal protein. Additionally, we also provide the resistant gene name, gene type, antibiotic resistance and resistance mechanism of the strain, for the use of visitors.

Why we are different

Our RPARS（Ribosomal proteins of antibiotic resistant strains）provides you with the resistance gene and strains ribosomal protein. As a user, you can glance over the strain which is antibiotic, download the fasta file of protein sequence, and even get the resisstance mechanism of the strain.
